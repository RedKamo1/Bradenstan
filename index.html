The Constitution of Bradenstan

In Order to Establish Domestic Tranquility...

Preamble

We, the people of Bradenstan, in order to establish domestic tranquility, ensure the defense of our sovereign borders (specifically, the entrance to the Room of the Sovereign Leader, hereinafter referred to as "The Room"), and secure the Blessings of Privacy to ourselves and our Posterity, do ordain and establish this Constitution for the Nation of Bradenstan.

Article I: Declaration of Borders and Sovereignty

Section 1: Definition of the Realm
The sovereign territory of Bradenstan is defined exclusively as the physical space within the four walls, floor, and ceiling of The Room, including the immediate airspace above and the space below.

Section 2: The Border
The sacred and respected border of Bradenstan is the plane of the closed door to The Room. Crossing this border requires permission.

Section 3: Territorial Integrity
The territorial integrity of Bradenstan is to be respected. Entry into Bradenstan is by express invitation from the Sovereign Leader.

Article II: Rights of the Sovereign Leader

Section 1: Right to Privacy
The Sovereign Leader of Bradenstan possesses a right to privacy within The Room.

Section 2: Right to Uninterrupted Enjoyment
The Sovereign Leader has the right to enjoy The Room without sudden entry or disruptions.

Article III: Respecting Boundaries

Section 1: The Principle of Respect
Respect for the boundaries of Bradenstan is expected from all, including Corry.

Section 2: Rules for Entry
Entry into The Room is permitted only with explicit invitation or by following established rules, such as a specific knocking sequence.
